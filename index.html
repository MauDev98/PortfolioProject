About me

I am a 23 years old peruvian business manager and self-taught web developer currentlty based in Canada.

I graduated as a bachelor in Business Management at the beginning of the year 2021, however by that time my interests had shifted.

Computers have always seemed like fantastic machines to me, I have had one for most of my life but to be honest I used it mainly for video games. I considered everything related to programming or software development as rocket science.

The Covid-19 pandemic changed the world for everyone and it certainly changed mine, I was locked up at home with a lot of free time and my university studies were almost finished when I saw an ad about a python course, I said to myself, why not?

This is the way I began the most amusing learning journey I have ever had, I learn the frustration of not understanding why the code did not do what I wanted and the joy when it did, I discovered a new world that I have no plans on letting go.
