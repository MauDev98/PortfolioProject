About me

I am a 23 years old peruvian business manager and self-taught web developer currentlty based in Canada.

I graduated as a bachelor in Business Management at the beginning of the year 2021, however by that time my interests had shifted.

Computers have always seemed like fantastic machines to me, I have had one for most of my life but to be honest I used it mainly for video games. I considered everything related to programming or software development as rocket science and I kept my distance.

The Covid-19 pandemic changed the world for everyone and I was not going to be the exception, I was locked up at home with a lot of free time and my university studies almost finished when I saw an ad about a python course, I said to myself, why not?

In this way I began the most fun learning journey I have ever had, I discovered about the frustration of not understanding why the code did not do what I wanted and the joy when it did, I discovered a new world that I will not let go
